COUGH

A cough, also known as tussis, is a voluntary or involuntary act that clears the throat and breathing passage of foreign particles, microbes, irritants, fluids, and mucus; it is a rapid expulsion of air from the lungs.
SYMPTOMS

A person suffering from this disease displays the following symptoms:

 1.Frequent dry cough
2.Phlegm/sputum production
3.Burning sensation or suffocation in the chest
4.Fever
5.Pain in the region of heart, temples, head, abdomen, sides
6.Irritability
7.Dryness in the mouth, excessive thirst, bitter taste
8.Sore throat, hoarseness of voice
9.Difficulty in swallowing food
10.Tiredness, weakness
11.Nausea after eating food.
